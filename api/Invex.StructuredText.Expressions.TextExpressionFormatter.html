Remarks

Format(TextExpression?) is implemented as a rewrite loop: RawExpression yields its text, ConcatExpression concatenates its formatted parts, and CastExpression<TTo> is unwrapped transparently. Every other node is passed to Resolve(TextExpression), which must rewrite it into a simpler expression; the loop repeats until everything bottoms out in raw text.
