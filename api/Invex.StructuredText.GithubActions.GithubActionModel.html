Permissions

GITHUB_TOKEN permissions at workflow or job level (permissions). This is a discriminated union: use Permissions.All to grant one level to every scope (read-all / write-all), or Permissions.Exact to specify levels per scope.

Permissions.All

Grants the same Level to every permission scope, rendered as read-all or write-all.

Permissions.Exact

Grants explicit levels for individual permission scopes.
